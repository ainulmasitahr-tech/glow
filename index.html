Core Interaction Tasks

3 Evaluated User Tasks

Each task was designed around real user needs identified during our research phase, then prototyped and tested for usability.

📅
01

Book an Appointment

Facial · Haircut

Users can browse featured beauty services, select a preferred date and time slot, and confirm a booking — all within a clean, guided flow.

  • Select a service (Facial, Hair Cut & Style, Massage, etc.)
  • Choose a preferred date on the calendar
  • Pick an available time slot
  • Confirm & receive booking summary
🛍️
02

Browse & Purchase Products

Skincare · Makeup · Hair

A curated shop experience lets users filter by category, view product ratings, and add items to cart seamlessly.

  • Open the Shop tab and search or filter by category
  • Browse product cards (rating, price, images)
  • Tap 'Buy Now' to add to cart
  • Proceed to checkout & payment
✨
03

Profile & Beauty Diary

Tracking · Personalisation

Users can manage their account, view appointment history, and maintain a personal Beauty Diary to track their skincare and wellness journey.

  • Access Account tab to view profile
  • Review appointment count & diary entries
  • Switch to 'Beauty Diary' tab
  • Log new entries, track routines & progress
